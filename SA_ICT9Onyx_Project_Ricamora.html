Fires happen anywhere and it's important to be prepared. It's important to look at all the ways you can prevent one.

Very simple steps that could keep you safe, and if you start right now with these simple tips, you could prevent a problem before it happens.


How to Prevent Fire

Unplug Items You’re Not Using		 Too much electricity flowing through your house is a potential fire hazard. A lot of things in your house, may be turned off, but still consume electricity.

Use Surge Protectors		 The very least, you should keep all your electronics plugged intro surge protectors. It keeps extra electricity out and reduce the risk of having a fire.

Keep Flammable Items Away From Heat		 Flammable things like paper or fabric should kept away from too much heat.

Don’t Smoke In the Home		 If you do, do it in a well-ventilated area and to put of the cigarette before throwing it away. There's a lot of materials inside of them that could burn if even if you've put them out.
